Shufflepuck: A Mobile Game by Ethan Anderson

Shufflepuck is a casual indie mobile game based on the classic board game Shuffleboard, with some elements inspired by curling.

Face 3 unique CPU difficulties to unlock new skins, and play against your friends locally or online!

Shufflepuck has no advertisements, no in-app purchases, and no data tracking.

Shufflepuck mobile game is available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, links below.

Many Ways to Play!

You can play Shufflepuck in a variety of ways. Battle against the CPU on easy, medium, or hard difficulties.

You can also play Shufflepuck with your friends. Play online by creating a lobby, then share the lobby code with your friend so they can join!

If you only have one mobile device between you, try local play! Take turns with the phone to challenge each other.

Unlock Cool Skins!

Shufflepuck has dozens of different cosmetic skins you can unlock and play with.

All skins can be unlocked for free, some via skill, like beating the CPU by a certain number of points, and some via luck, from the Shuffleplink minigame!

Try to collect all the skins! Show off your rare skins in games against your friends!

Collect Rare Cards!

Shufflepuck is also a deck building strategy mobile game!

There are 5 rarities of cards: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. High rarity cards have more powerful effects, so try to collect them all!

All cards also have special variations. For example, you can discover different ranks of cards like Bronze, Gold, and Diamond. Show off your rare cards in game!

Build Crazy Decks!

After you collect all the cards, put your brains to the test and build a strong deck to defeat your opponents!

Each turn before you shoot, you'll have a chance to play cards. Use them to get more points, disrupt your opponent, or even destroy other pucks!

Some cards work better with others, so try out different combinations to achieve different strategies to win!

Join the discord to share your decklists with others!
